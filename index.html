The Social Impact Club

The Social Impact Club (SIC) was established within Curtin University Colombo by passionate and driven individuals seeking to create a profound and lasting impact within the university community and broader society.

Sri Lanka, like many developing countries, confronts significant social inequalities, and amidst these challenges, there is an urgent need for transformative action. SIC firmly believes that the university community, as a source of knowledge, creativity, and enthusiasm, is vital in shaping a brighter future.

Through meaningful community service initiatives and charity drives, SIC seeks to close the gap between privilege and need, facilitating a sense of shared humanity. With our dynamic and collaborative approach aligned with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, we are committed to a holistic approach that addresses immediate challenges and empowers students to be catalysts of change
